What is ISO 9001:2015?
ISO 9001:2015 is an international standard that establishes the criteria for a quality management system.	[[https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/compliance/offerings/offering-iso-9001|[ref 1]]]

What principles form the basis for ISO 9001:2015?
The standard is based on several quality management principles:
1. clear focus on meeting customer requirements
2. strong corporate governance and leadership commitment to quality objectives
3. process-driven approach to meeting objectives
4. focus on +continous_improvement
