What is Continuous Testing?
Continuous Testing is the process of executing automated tests as part of the software delivery pipeline 
in order to obtain feedback on the business risks associated with a softare release candidate as rapidly as possible. [[https://www.tricentis.com/products/what-is-continuous-testing/|[ref 1]]]
