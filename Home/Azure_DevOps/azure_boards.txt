What is Azure Boards?
Azure Boards is an Azure service for managing the workflow of software projects.

What do you use Azure Boards to accomplish?
Plan work.	[[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbFKi6Hflc0|[ref 1]]]

What are 4 very important functions of Azure Boards?	[[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbFKi6Hflc0|[ref 1]]]
* Plan your work with Azure Boards
* Build your board and backlog
* Run a sprint
* Track important work
