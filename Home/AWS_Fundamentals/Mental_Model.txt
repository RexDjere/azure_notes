What is the Mental Model?	[[https://aws.amazon.com/getting-started/fundamentals-core-concepts/|[ref 1]]]
* When thinking about operational excellence in the cloud, it is useful to think of it in terms of [[+automation]] .
* [[+Human_error]] is the primary cause of defects and operational incidents.
* The more operations that can be automated, the less chance there is for human error.
* In addition to preventing error, automation helps you to continuously improve your internal processes.
* They promote a set of repeatable best practices that can be applied across your entire organization.
