What does REST stand for?
REST is an acronym for REpresentational State Transfer.

Who created the concept of REST?
[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roy_Fielding|Roy Fielding]]

What is representational state transfer?
Representational state transfer (REST) is a softwarer architectural style that was created to guide the design and development of the architect for the World WIde Web.

What is the title of Roy Fielding's dissertation that introdued the concept of REST?
Architectural Styles and the Design of Network-based Software Architectures
