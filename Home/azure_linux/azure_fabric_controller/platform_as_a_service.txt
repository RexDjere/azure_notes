What is platform as a service?
Platform as a service (PaaS) is a category of cloud computing services that allows customers to provision, instantiate, run, and manage 
a modular bundle comprising a computing platform and one or more applications, without the complexity of building and maintaining 
the infastructure typically asssociated with developing and launching the application(s); and to allow developers to create, develop, 
and package such software bundles. [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Platform_as_a_service|[ref 1]]]

What does Paas deliver?

What are the advantages of PaaS? [[https://www.bmc.com/blogs/saas-vs-paas-vs-iaas-whats-the-difference-and-how-to-choose/#ref2|[ref 2]]]
* simplicity
* scalability
* high availability
* developers can customize apps
* reduction in coding
* automation of business policy
* easy migration to hybrid model

What is PaaS?
Platform as a service (PaaS) is a complete development and deployment environment in the cloud. [[https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/overview/what-is-paas/|[ref 3]]]
